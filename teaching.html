MATH 27300: Basic Theory of $p$-adic Numbers

Course Description & Syllabus

This course provides a rigorous introduction to the arithmetic of $p$-adic numbers. We begin with the construction of $\mathbb{Q}_p$ as the completion of $\mathbb{Q}$ with respect to the $p$-adic absolute value and explore its unique topological properties (e.g., the ultrametric inequality and compactness of $\mathbb{Z}_p$).

Key Topics:

  • Hensel's Lemma and local root finding.
  • The structure of the multiplicative group $\mathbb{Q}_p^\times$.
  • Introduction to $p$-adic analysis and Mahler series.
  • Brief overview of $p$-adic differential equations (if time permits).
